Our Journey

The JobPortal Story

From idea to Kosovo's leading job platform

2024
The Idea Was Born

Recognizing the gap in Kosovo's job market, we envisioned a platform that would connect talented individuals with leading companies efficiently and transparently.

2025
Platform Launch

JobPortal officially launched with our first partner companies and hundreds of job listings, marking the beginning of our mission to transform job searching in Kosovo.

2026
Growing Strong

Today, we serve thousands of job seekers and dozens of companies, with new features like our Resume Builder helping users present their best selves to employers.

Future
What's Next

We're constantly innovating—AI-powered job matching, mobile apps, and expansion across the Balkans are on our roadmap. The best is yet to come!
